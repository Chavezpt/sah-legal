Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026 | Last Updated: February 24, 2026

State of the Art Healing LLC ("SAH," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and confidentiality of all information we collect. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect information in connection with our internal software tools and integrations, including our QuickBooks Online integration used solely for internal accounting and financial management purposes.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the internal software application developed and operated by State of the Art Healing LLC for the purpose of accessing and managing our own QuickBooks Online financial data. This application is not a public-facing product and is not distributed to third parties.

2. Information We Access

Through our QuickBooks Online integration, we access financial data belonging solely to State of the Art Healing LLC, including but not limited to: income and expense records, accounts receivable, accounts payable, payroll data, and financial reports. We do not collect, store, or share personal financial information belonging to any third party through this integration.

3. How We Use Information

Financial data accessed through our QuickBooks integration is used exclusively for internal business management, reporting, and operational decision-making. We do not sell, rent, or share this data with any third parties for marketing or commercial purposes.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect the data we access, including secure storage of authentication credentials, encrypted connections for all data transmission, and restricted access limited to authorized personnel only. All data is processed locally on our secure internal systems.

5. HIPAA Compliance

State of the Art Healing LLC operates in the behavioral health and recovery services industry and is committed to full compliance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA). Our QuickBooks integration accesses only financial and accounting data and does not involve any protected health information (PHI).

6. Data Retention

Financial data accessed through our QuickBooks integration is retained in accordance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements and our internal data retention policies. We retain financial records for a minimum of seven years as required by law.

7. Third-Party Services

Our QuickBooks integration uses Intuit's QuickBooks Online API and is subject to Intuit's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy in addition to this policy. We do not share your data with any other third-party services through this integration.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page.
